The Tree of Life (Terrence Malick)

La La Land (Damien Chazelle)

La La Land is a 2016 American musical romantic comedy film written and directed by Damien Chazelle. It stars Ryan Gosling as a jazz pianist and Emma Stone as an aspiring actress, who meet and fall in love in Los Angeles while pursuing their dreams.

Kill Bill is a 2003 American martial arts film written and directed by Quentin Tarantino. Fox) and their leader Bill (David Carradine) after they try to kill her and her unborn child.
